FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Why is a Groq API key required? ›
X-Brain uses Groq's AI models to generate comments. Groq offers a free API — 14,400 requests/day, no credit card required. You can get your API key at console.groq.com in under a minute. The key is stored only in your browser and never sent to any server.
Will my X account get banned? ›
X-Brain uses human behavior simulation — random wait times between comments, natural scroll movements, and Gaussian-distributed delays. Daily limits and spam protection mechanisms protect your account. However, as with any automation tool, excessive use carries risk — following recommended limits is important.
Can I cancel anytime? ›
Yes, you can cancel at any time. If you purchased a yearly plan and are not satisfied within 30 days, you will receive a full refund. After cancellation, you can continue using Pro features until the end of your billing period.
How many devices does the license work on? ›
Your Pro license is for personal use and activates on a single Chrome browser. Separate licenses are required for multiple profiles or devices.
Is my payment information secure? ›
All payments are processed through Lemon Squeezy infrastructure — PCI DSS compliant, industry-standard security. Your credit card information is never stored in our systems.
